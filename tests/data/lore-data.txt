Zorvath the Perpetually Confused is a semi-corporeal librarian from the 7½th
dimension who accidentally invented gravity twice while trying to organize a
collection of sentient spoons. Born during a clerical error in the cosmic
registry of existence, Zorvath speaks exclusively in footnotes and insists
that time moves “sideways on alternating Thursdays.” After misfiling an
entire galaxy under “miscellaneous,” he was sentenced by the Interdimensional
Council to wander realities cataloging paradoxes that may or may not exist.
Despite possessing infinite knowledge of things that are mostly irrelevant,
Zorvath cannot remember his own name without consulting a laminated card he
keeps losing. He is often accompanied by a small, judgmental cloud named
Bernice, who rains selectively on philosophical discussions she finds
unconvincing.
